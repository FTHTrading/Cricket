## Revised Capital-Markets Philosophy

The current framework replaces this stack with a different logic:

**Old logic:** Raise capital around an asset (the stadium) to build the operating business.

**New logic:** Build operating businesses first. Only then consider whether specific revenue streams are legally and commercially suitable to package into ancillary capital structures.

The current platform targets ancillary SPVs of $1M–$10M each, specifically for revenue streams that are:
- Already generating cash flow
- Legally ring-fenced with documented operating rights
- Supported by at least 12 months of auditable financial history

This is a fundamentally different standard — and a fundamentally more credible one.
