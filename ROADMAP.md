## Phase 3 — Pilot Programs
**Duration:** Weeks 8–16 · **Status:** Not Started

**Objective:** Launch at least one live module with real operating data.

| Pilot | Description | Target | Success Criteria |
|-------|-------------|--------|-----------------|
| Sponsorship reporting | Live sponsor tracking, billing, and performance reports | APL sponsor or league sponsor | One full event or season of sponsor data produced |
| Event settlement | End-to-end settlement for one tournament or event day | APL or metro club event | Settlement report and reconciliation output delivered |
| Membership wallet | Founding-member or premium-pass digital accounts | 50–200 members | Active accounts with recurring payment records |
| League registration and payments | Online team and player registration with fee collection | Metro club or APL season | Completed registrations and payment records |

**Exit gate:** At least one module is live, processing real transactions, generating real operating data.  
**Dependencies:** Phase 2 product specs complete; at least one confirmed partner.
