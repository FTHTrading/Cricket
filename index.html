The Platform

Three Layers. One Integrated Operating System.

Each layer solves a distinct problem. Together they create the commercial infrastructure that makes Georgia cricket a serious business.

🏏
Layer 1 — Now
League OS

The administrative and financial operating system for league cricket — registration, payments, scheduling, compliance, and community.

  • Club and player registration with RBAC
  • Fee collection and payment routing
  • Season and match scheduling
  • Membership tiers and wallets
  • Audit logs and compliance reporting
🏟️
Layer 2 — Phase 3+
Venue OS

The commercial operating system for professional cricket venues — ticketing, concessions, hospitality, sponsorship, and real-time settlement.

  • Ticketing and gate management
  • Concession and vendor settlement
  • Hospitality suite inventory
  • Sponsorship activation tracking
  • Event-by-event P&L dashboard
📊
Layer 3 — Later Stage
Capital Markets OS

Ancillary capital structures for specific, rights-gated revenue streams once real operating data and secured rights make structured financing appropriate.

  • SPV structuring for revenue streams
  • ERC-3643 digital securities reference
  • Reg D 506(c) compliance framework
  • Investor reporting and distributions
  • Gated behind operating proof — not the lead story

The Opportunity

Why FutureTech. Why Georgia. Why Now.

Georgia is one of the fastest-growing cricket markets in the United States. The infrastructure to support professional operations does not yet exist. That is the opportunity.

01
FTH Is Already in the Market
FutureTech Holding Company operates at 5655 Peachtree Parkway, Peachtree Corners, Georgia. Atlanta Premier League LLC operates from the same address. FutureTech Lions are active in the Atlanta cricket league. This is not an outside investor — it is an existing operator.
02
LaGrange Cricket Grounds
LaGrange Cricket Grounds broke ground in November 2025 and is targeting a Q1 2027 opening — the first purpose-built professional cricket facility in Georgia. FutureTech is positioning Venue OS as the operating layer for this and future venues.
03
A Market Without Operating Infrastructure
Metro Atlanta has multiple cricket leagues, clubs, and a growing South Asian diaspora population. None of these organizations currently have access to purpose-built payment, registration, or venue management systems built for cricket.
04
12 Revenue Streams, Waiting to Be Built
Registration fees, membership dues, sponsorships, ticketing, concessions, hospitality, media rights, academy programs, equipment retail, digital content, private events, and ancillary capital structures — all currently uncaptured.
05
Operate First. Package Later.
FutureTech's governing principle: build real operations, generate real data, secure real rights. Only then does structured financing become appropriate. The platform is sequenced correctly. Capital markets activity is gated, not assumed.
06
Partner-Friendly by Design
FTH brings technology, operating systems, and commercial infrastructure. Partners bring venues, rights, and community relationships. The commercial product comes from combining them — not from FTH claiming what it does not own.
